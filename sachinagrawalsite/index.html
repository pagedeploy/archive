My Game, Attack on the Ewoks:

Introduction:

Long long ago, in a galaxy far far away, there lived a village of Ewoks! But the Ewoks are under attack from the Dark Side. TIE Fighters are attacking the village. Your job is to shoot all the TIE Fighters from your X-Wing. Good luck, Jedi Master.

Controls:

  • W: Move Up
  • A: Rapid Fire
  • S: Move Down
  • D: Fire

Other Information:

You loose if you come in contact with a TIE Fighter. Once your score is 2000, you win.

My Quiz, The Impossible Minecraft Name Quiz:

Information:

This quiz will ask you to choose between two options for the name of a Minecraft block. You have to try your best to choose the correct one. It may require a little effort, as some blocks may be harder than others.

Instructions:

  • Select the answer you think is correct.
  • Move onto the next question.
  • Repeat the steps above for the entire quiz.
  • Your answers will be graded on the spot.
  • Press the submit button once finished.
  • There will be some snow and a disco background.
